This is your chance to see Los Angeles beyond the tourist lens and explore the worlds of Art, Music, Film & Food. Students will gain an appreciation for art and will experience a behind-the-scenes world in the entertainment industry. We will take advantage of LA’s diverse food town, enjoy dinner at hip restaurants and take in cooking classes at Eataly LA. We will go to art museums including the Getty Center in West LA and the Getty Villa in Malibu. We will finish the trip with tours of Paramount and Warner Brothers studios. Art, music, entertainment, and food are all wrapped up in amazing Southern California EBC trip–join us!
