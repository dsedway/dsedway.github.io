My thoughts on basketball shoes

I have been playing basketball since I was 4. I have always loved the culture and the obsession around vintage and current basketball shoes. Every basketball shoe needs to have 3 key components in order to be a good or great shoe. First, it has to have good cushion. The Air Jordan 1s, seen here had great traction, so they would stick to the ground, but the cushion was terrible. Michael Jordan said that his feet would bleed in them due to the sole feeling like cardboard. A great basketball shoe must have great traction. Like I previously mentioned, a basketball shoe needs to stick to the ground. If I'm slipping, I can get injured and be slow on the court. Last, but not least, a basketball shoe needs to look good. A distracting and absurd silhouette for a shoe frustrates buyers and wearers. Good performing basketball shoes usually look good. A clean and simple design indicates a great shoe.
